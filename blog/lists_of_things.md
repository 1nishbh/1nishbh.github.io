if one sets out to make a personal list of things that matter to them, i wonder what would be the ones worth curating.

for instance, **list of** ---\
1. books to read, books read, most re-read books, self-authored best poems\
2. movies to watch, albums to listen, playlists\
3. favorite artists, scientists, politicians, activists, writers\
4. questions to ask oneself, unsolved problems, biases ...

let's consider this list, for exaxple, [Those Weirdos Who Help Make Life Worth Living](https://rateyourmusic.com/list/hellaguru/those-weirdos-who-help-make-life-worth-living/) on RYM
